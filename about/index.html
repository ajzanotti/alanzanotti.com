About

I've been professionally administering and developing on Linux systems since 2011.

The majority of my experience is with enterprise Linux and variants, namely Red Hat and Oracle Linux. I've worked extensively with popular open source solutions such as Apache HTTP Server, Apache Tomcat, and MySQL. I'm also familiar with several products in the Oracle stack including Oracle Database and Oracle WebLogic.

Every organization is different, but I'm a strong proponent for the use of agile methodologies. Now more than ever it's important for IT departments and development teams to connect with their customers to deliver value. I believe in the benefits of repeatable, measurable processes and leadership that doesn't lord over its employees. Version control should be a staple in every environment and best practice should be a reality and not a lofty ideal.
